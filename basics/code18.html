Google Fonts

Google Fonts is a free and open-source web font service provided by Google. It allows designers and developers to use a wide variety of fonts in their websites and applications with minimal effort. The service is fast, reliable, and easy to integrate via a simple `< link / >` in HTML. With a vast collection of typefaces like Montserrat, Oswald, Roboto, and more, Google Fonts helps bring beautiful typography to the web without compromising performance.
